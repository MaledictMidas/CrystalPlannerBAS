# CRYSTAL SELECTION
Each of the three slots can hold one Crystal, just like the base game.
• Choose from the five default Crystals.
• Create and use your own custom Crystals.
• The same Crystal cannot be equipped twice.
• You can freely swap between Crystals without losing your work.

## Creation Orb
This orb is used for creating Subclass skills by right-clicking on it.
- The **Green dot** in the 1st ring is for Tier 1, 2nd ring for Tier 2, 3rd ring for Tier 3.

<img width="652" height="580" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2481693b-0cc1-4d0c-8ebd-957fa4a5545a" />

### After Right-Clicking:
Here you can plan out your Sub Skill:

<img width="597" height="519" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/84d28ce3-f67f-4d9d-9476-39e0da5ef643" />


- **Name:**
- **Description:**
- **UnlockCost:** How many Shards it costs.
- **Refund Value:** How many Shards you get back if you refund.

At the end, press **Add Subskill** to save.
*Note: Cancelling or clicking X will not save your progress!*
