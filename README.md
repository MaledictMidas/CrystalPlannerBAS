## Introduction
This is the first thing you will see when opening the HTML file. Just like in the game, you will see 3 slots.

<img width="1917" height="939" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2671fa20-abf6-4fb3-8ab1-3990fc956a2d" />

> ### ⚠️ Warning:
> Refreshing the HTML page deletes your progress. 
> - Always **Load & Save** your progress with the Upload and Download buttons.
> - A Template for all the B&S Spells can be found in the Repository: ``monolith-spell-BAS-Template.json``

### (The Download app button is just the download for the HTML)

You see these 3 slots; in each, you insert one of 5 crystals as in the base game.
- You **can** add custom crystals as well at the bottom.
- You **can't** use the same Crystal twice.
- You **can** swap between Crystals without losing progress.

## Creation Orb
This orb is used for creating Subclass skills by right-clicking on it.
- The **Green dot** in the 1st ring is for Tier 1, 2nd ring for Tier 2, 3rd ring for Tier 3.

<img width="652" height="580" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2481693b-0cc1-4d0c-8ebd-957fa4a5545a" />

### After Right-Clicking:
Here you can plan out your Sub Skill:
<img width="597" height="519" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/84d28ce3-f67f-4d9d-9476-39e0da5ef643" />

- **Name:**
- **Description:**
- **UnlockCost:** How many Shards it costs.
- **Refund Value:** How many Shards you get back if you refund.

At the end, press **Add Subskill** to save.
*Note: Cancelling or clicking X will not save your progress!*
